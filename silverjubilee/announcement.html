What's This About?

July 19th, 2022

Maaret Pyhäjärvi is an exploratory tester extraordinaire and a polyglot programmer from Finland. She is a community learner and finds people to learn with by speaking at conferences on a variety of software topics. October 2022 she is celebrating her 25th career anniversary, and thought there is one fitting way to do so: organizing an event for the community.

Anyone is welcome to attend the online events. They are twofold:
  • A Conference Day of Talks - Maaret hosts speakers on career and professionalism
  • Workshops - Maaret teaches online workshops
